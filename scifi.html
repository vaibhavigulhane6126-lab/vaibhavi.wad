SCI-FI MOVIE

Sci‑fi movies explore futuristic technology, space travel, artificial intelligence, and alternate realities, often reflecting human hopes and fears about progress. They mix imagination with science to ask “what if?” about our future.
Classic Sci‑Fi
2001: A Space Odyssey (1968) – A mysterious black monolith influences human evolution, while astronauts face the rogue AI HAL 9000.
Star Wars: A New Hope (1977) – Luke Skywalker joins rebels to fight the Empire and bring hope to the galaxy.
Blade Runner (1982) – A detective hunts synthetic humans called replicants, questioning what it means to be truly human.
The Matrix (1999) – A hacker discovers reality is a computer simulation controlled by machines.

Modern Sci‑Fi
Inception (2010) – Thieves enter dreams to steal secrets, blurring the line between reality and imagination.
Interstellar (2014) – Astronauts travel through a wormhole to find a new home for humanity.
Arrival (2016) – A linguist deciphers alien language, uncovering a new way of perceiving time.
Ex Machina (2015) – A programmer tests a humanoid AI’s consciousness, leading to unsettling consequences.

Recent Sci‑Fi
Dune: Part One (2021) – Paul Atreides must embrace his destiny on the desert planet Arrakis.
The Creator (2023) – In a war between humans and AI, a soldier discovers a childlike android with immense power.
