ROMANCE MOVIE

Some of the most beloved romance movies include Titanic, The Notebook, La La Land, Pride & Prejudice, and Before Sunrise. These films capture the magic, heartbreak, and intensity of love stories across different eras and styles.
What Makes Romance Movies Special
Emotional connection: They explore love, longing, heartbreak, and passion.
Grand gestures & meet-cutes: Iconic moments like Jack and Rose on the Titanic or Noah building the house in The Notebook.
Timeless appeal: Romance films often become cultural touchstones, revisited across generations.

Why People Love Them
Relatable emotions: Everyone connects with themes of love, loss, and hope.
Beautiful settings: From Vienna streets (Before Sunrise) to lavish ships (Titanic).
Memorable performances: Actors often deliver career-defining roles in romance films.

Modern Favorites
Titanic (1997) – Jack and Rose’s tragic love aboard the ill-fated ship.
The Notebook (2004) – A sweeping romance spanning decades.
La La Land (2016) – Musical romance about love and ambition in Los Angeles.

Unique & Indie
Before Sunrise (1995) – Two strangers connect deeply during one night in Vienna.
500 Days of Summer (2009) – A realistic look at love and heartbreak.
Call Me by Your Name (2017) – A tender coming-of-age romance set in Italy.

Bollywood Romance
Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge (1995) – One of India’s most iconic love stories.
Kabir Singh (2019) – Intense romance with themes of passion and obsession.
Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani (2013) – Friendship and love
