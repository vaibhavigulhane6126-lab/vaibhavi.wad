TYPES OF MOVIES

    Movie types (genres) are categories based on shared
storytelling elements, moods, or subjects, including major ones like
Comedy,Drama, Action, Horror, Sci-Fi, Romance, and Thriller, plus
documentaries (real events), animation (drawn/CGI), and biographical films
(real people's lives). They can also be
mixed, like Action-Comedy, or focus on style (Art Film) or structure (Anthology).
